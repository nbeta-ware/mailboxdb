DBMAIL-SIEVED(8)
================

NAME
----
dbmail-sieved - speaks the Manage Sieve draft protocol, used for managing
Sieve scripts for a user's email account.

SYNOPSIS
--------
dbmail-sieved [-f configfile] [-p pidfile] [-nvVh]

DESCRIPTION
-----------
The dbmail-sieved process listens, by default, on port 2000
of the localhost interface.
